About This App

This application provides current and historical gold and silver rates for Chennai, Tamil Nadu. Rates are sourced from reliable metal price APIs and updated regularly.

  • Gold 24K: 99.9% purity gold
  • Gold 22K: 91.6% purity gold (commonly used in jewelry)
  • Silver: 99.9% purity silver

Note: Actual rates may vary slightly depending on the jeweler and location. These rates are indicative and should be verified before making any transactions.
